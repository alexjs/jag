# Troubleshooting log

Symptom → investigation → outcome, newest first. This is the highest-value
content in the repo for other owners (and for future-me): what it looked
like, what it turned out to be, and what red herrings were chased.

Template:

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — Symptom in one line
- **Symptom:** what was observed, under what conditions (hot/cold, idle/load)
- **Suspected:** initial hypotheses
- **Checked:** tests done and readings, with corpus refs (Palm p. N / ROM op N)
- **Cause:**
- **Fix:** (cross-reference maintenance/log.md entry)
- **Red herrings:**
```
