# Known V12 HE issues — watchlist

The usual XJS V12 weak points, weighted for a car living in 30°C+ humidity
year-round. Status column tracks this car specifically.

| Issue | Why it matters in Singapore | Status on this car |
|---|---|---|
| Cooling system marginal by design | Tropical heat + traffic crawl is the worst case. Overheating → dropped valve seats → engine out. Radiator, both fans, thermostats, and coolant bleed all need to be right | ☐ to assess |
| Fuel hoses in the engine V | Heat-aged rubber over the ignition amplifier is the classic XJS fire. Replace on age, not condition | ☐ to assess |
| Ignition amplifier heat soak | Sits in the V; failure = sudden cutout, often when hot | ☐ to assess |
| Half-shaft inner U-joints & rear brakes | Inboard rear discs are easy to neglect | ☐ to assess |
| Electrics / grounds | Humidity accelerates corrosion at ground points | ☐ to assess |
| Air-con performance | Not a luxury here. Original system marginal even when new | ☐ to assess |
