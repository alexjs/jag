# Singapore-specific notes

Running a 1985 car in Singapore is as much a regulatory exercise as a
mechanical one. Notes here on the schemes, inspections, and local ecosystem.
**Rules change — verify against LTA/OneMotoring before acting on anything
here, and PRs/corrections welcome.**

## Registration scheme

A 1985 car is eligible for the **Classic Vehicle Scheme** (vehicles ≥ 35 years
old). Things to document for this car:

- [ ] Which scheme the car is on (normal COE / Classic / Vintage)
- [ ] Usage restrictions and day-licence rules under the current scheme
- [ ] Road tax and inspection cadence as applied to this car
- [ ] COE renewal economics if on normal registration
